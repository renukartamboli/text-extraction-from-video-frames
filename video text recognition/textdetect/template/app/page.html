About Project

Now a days,there are many online education platforms for distance education worldwide that conduct correspondence courses.Theses courses are highly in the form of Video Tutorials or live streaming video lectures.Generally,we need to take notes manually with pen and paper from these streaming video lectures/tutorials or any other video source if pdf tutorial is not available for that video.Here our aim is to create an application to take notes from video sources ,digitally.User can play the video by inserting youtube video link or by uploading video from local storage.User can also upload simple text image.In the application,user can select paragraph,code,useful definition (region of interest) from streaming video or uploaded image.After selection of region of interest from video/image,text document for corresponding image/frame will be available for user.
